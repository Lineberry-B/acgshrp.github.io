About the Adams County Gold Star Honor Roll Project

The Adams County, Indiana WWII Gold Star Honor Roll is an ongoing project of the History Topics Course at Bellmont High School in Decatur, Indiana. All information has been researched and compiled by Bellmont students through a variety of local, government, and internet sources. Every effort has been made to include complete and accurate information.

Special thanks to our research partners:

Decatur-Adams County Public Library

Shana Neuenschwander of the Berne Library Heritage Room

and the Adams County Historical Museum
